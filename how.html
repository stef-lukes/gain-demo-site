How It Works...

Our expertise in social media makes it simple for us to organically grow your social media accounts. We use proven, tried and tested marketing techniques that produce trusted results. With engagement and professionally designed graphics, we drive targeted audiences directly to your social media profiles. Get started today and let us manage your accounts.

Step 1:
Set Up Your Dashboard

Once you have chosen your package, selected any add ons, and signed up online - you will immediately land on a webpage with our On-boarding Instructions. All you need to do is sign up to your new social dashboard and connect your social media accounts. If you don’t have any social media accounts set up, then we can create these for you at no extra charge.

Step 2:
The Research Phase

Every client is unique and that’s why we spend up to seven days researching your business and industry. We analyse leading competitors in your industry to find out what social media strategy will be affective. Once we have this data we will be in a stronger position to create your social media content.

Step 3:
Content Creation Phase

After completing our research we will begin the content creation phase, in which we will create your social media posts. You will be sent a preview before anything goes live. If you choose to add a Facebook/Instagram advertising budget to your package, then you will be sent a preview 7 to 14 days after your first social media post goes live.

Step 4:
Monthly Analytics

We produce a detailed monthly report to help you understand the ROI of our services. These reports will show you exactly how many people we have reached, how many followers/likes we have generated! And much more! If you need help we have a helpful video which will show you all you need to know about this section of your dashboard.
